The studio is led by Patricia Chen — a founder who grew up under a Calgary Stampede sky and turned a love of story and craft into a creative practice. What started in Alberta now runs across Western Canada, from Vancouver to Toronto.

We started AB Digilife because most marketing is forgettable — informational, safe, and gone the moment you scroll past. We make the opposite: work people stop for, remember, and act on. Marketing you can see, proven on camera.

Whether it's a brand born today or a business ready for its next chapter, we bring strategy, film, and growth under one roof — so nothing gets lost in translation between the idea and the screen.
