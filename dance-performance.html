Common questions

How much does a dance studio brand film cost in Vancouver? +

AB Digilife dance studio brand films are scoped by project — a half-day shoot covering one discipline includes filming, editing, and colour grade. Contact us for a brief.

What is included in recital or showcase day coverage? +

Showcase day coverage includes multi-camera filming of the full evening, a 3–5 minute keepsake highlight reel, and individual number cut-downs for each class or performer. Delivered within 7–10 days.

Can you edit a performance reel to specific music? +

Yes. All performer reels are cut to music you provide or selected collaboratively. We work to the beat and dynamics of the track, not a template.

How quickly is the edited video delivered after filming? +

Recital and showcase films are delivered within 7–10 business days. Brand films and performer reels typically take 2–3 weeks, depending on revision rounds.

Do you film dance studios outside Vancouver? +

Our primary base is Vancouver, with regular projects in Calgary, Edmonton, and Toronto. We take select travel engagements for showcase days, competitions, and brand films.
