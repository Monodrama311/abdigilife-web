Common questions

What is a founder brand film? +

A founder brand film is a short cinematic video that puts you at the centre of the story - capturing your vision, values, and voice in a way that builds trust with investors, partners, and clients. AB Digilife produces founder brand films in Vancouver for executives and personal brands across industries.

How do I build thought leadership content as a founder? +

Thought leadership content starts with identifying the ideas and expertise that only you can speak to. AB Digilife turns those ideas into a LinkedIn video series, short-form social content, and speaking reels - produced on a regular cadence so your audience grows over time.

Can you create Chinese-language personal brand content for Vancouver founders? +

Yes - bilingual personal brand content in English and Mandarin is a core part of our founder work. We produce content for 小红书, WeChat, LinkedIn, and Instagram that reaches both English-speaking and Chinese-Canadian professional audiences in Vancouver and beyond.

What is a LinkedIn video strategy for founders and executives? +

A LinkedIn video strategy combines regular short-form videos, thought leadership clips, and behind-the-scenes content to grow a professional audience. AB Digilife plans, shoots, edits, and publishes a consistent video cadence on LinkedIn so your profile stays active and authoritative.

How long does a founder personal brand project take? +

A founder brand film takes 2-3 weeks from brief to delivery. A LinkedIn or social content series is set up within 2 weeks and runs on a monthly shoot-and-publish rhythm. We scope the timeline to your goals and calendar during the first call.
